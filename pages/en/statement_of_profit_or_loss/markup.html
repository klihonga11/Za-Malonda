6.5 Markup

Markup is the amount added on top of the cost price to reach the selling price of a product.

If Thomas buys one sugarcane at K 100 but sells it at K 200, the markup on the sugarcane is 100% (K 100). This means that the sugarcane was sold for 100% more than what Thomas paid for it.

1.2 Objectives

Thomas, an entrepreneur, has spotted an opportunity to open a grocery store near his home and will call it “Thomas’s Grocery Store”. Throughout this course, you will be helping Thomas record his financial transactions. By the end of the course, you will have helped him:

  • Record his every day transactions into a general journal.
  • Post those transactions to a general ledger.
  • Create a trial balance.
  • Construct a statement of profit or loss.
  • Draw up a statement of financial position.

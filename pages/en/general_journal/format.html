2.2 Format

Page No: short for page number.

Date: the date on which the transaction took place.

Accounts: the name of the account.

  • The account to be debited must always be entered first, followed by the account to be credited.
  • As in the example, leave a space before entering the name of the account to be credited.
  • Also, enter a short description of the transaction after the two accounts have been entered.

Ref: short for reference.

  • You should enter the account number.
  • The account number is found in the general ledger, which you will see in the next chapter.

Debit: the amount of money that has been debited to the account.

Credit: the amount of money that has been credited to the account.


Note: If you use specialized journals to record your transactions, they will all look and work the same way. Its just about what you record in them.

4.2 Format

Account Name: the name of the account.

Account No.: short for account number.

  • You can choose your own account numbers.
  • The account numbers should never change.
  • You should keep a list of your businessâ€™s account names with their account numbers. This is known as a chart of accounts.
  • This is the account number you record in the general journal.

Date: the date on which the transaction took place.

Description: a description of the transaction.

  • Enter the name of the other account in the transaction.

Ref: short for reference.

  • You should enter the page number of the journal where the transaction was first entered.
  • This is used so you can go back and find the original entry in the general journal.


Note: Remember, the left side of the account is for debit entries while the right side is for credit entries. This makes an account look like a 'T' and so accounts are also known as a T accounts.

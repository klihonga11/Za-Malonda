4.4 Exercise

On 1 January 2020, Thomas decides to start his business and puts in K 10,000 of his own money as capital.

On 3 January 2020, Thomas buys 25 sugarcane at K 100 each.

He pays K 500 for transport in order to buy the sugarcane.

On 4 January, he buys a plastic bucket to store his sugarcane for K 1,000. He receives a receipt with the receipt number 1225.

He decides to be selling the sugarcane at K 150 each.

On 5 January, he sells 10 sugarcane.

On 11 January, he sells 4 sugarcane.

On 22 January, he sells 6 sugarcane.

On 29 January, he sells 5 sugarcane.

All transactions are made using cash.


Using the information above, copy Thomas's transactions for the month of January 2020 from the general journal to a general ledger.
